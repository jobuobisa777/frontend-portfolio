About Us


It all started with a vision...
Our founder David was a successful entrepeneur in the gas station and car wash industry. He had seen it all, but wanted more...


HAving been around the world, David experienced firsthand the pressing issue of climate change. He wondered to himseld, "How can my industry adapt to help solve the world's problems?"
Then, lightning stuck -
An eco friendly car wash that contributes to global causes and saves water, while being a great experience and bringing awarness to global causes!


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to help heal the planet by offering excellent car washes that save water, use responsible materials, and bring awareness to the global water shortage .


VISION: The environment is important to us. That’s why we are Blue Coral Beyond Green certified and use exclusively biodegradable cleaning solutions. All of our products are earth-friendly and our innovative equipment comprehensively cleans cars with less water. We have also implemented a practice called ‘reclamation,’ a mechanical system that removes dirt, oil and grease from water in the car wash process. This innovative technique transforms water that we reuse throughout the wash process.
